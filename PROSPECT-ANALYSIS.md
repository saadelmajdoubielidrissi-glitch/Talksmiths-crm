## Company Profile

### Overview
Chari is a Moroccan fintech and B2B e-commerce platform founded in 2020 by husband-and-wife team Ismael Belkhayat (CEO) and Sophia Alj (COO). The company enables traditional retailers to order FMCG products via mobile app with free delivery within hours. Chari holds a payment institution license from Bank Al-Maghrib—making it the first VC-backed startup in Morocco to obtain this license.

### Business Model
- **Primary:** B2B e-commerce platform for retailers (25,000+ merchants)
